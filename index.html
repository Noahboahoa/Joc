Maus Review

Title description, Dec 7, 2017

Maus is a graphic novel 2 part series that uncovers the trajedies of ww2. Focusing on the holocaust, it highlights the tradjedies of many perspectives. Inspired by his holocaust survivor father's stories, the author writes his account in his book to portray his legacy. Although, some may consider the book controversial. I reccomend this book if you read it havent already.
